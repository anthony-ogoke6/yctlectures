Our Policy


Seller

* You must register(sign up) before you can upload your product on yctmaket.

* As we showcase your product to a large customer base, we get 5% on every sale for each product uploaded. This is to ensure that you state the appropriate amount when uploading your product on yctmarket.

* Your product(s) will be displayed free on yctmarket for a period of one month, after which it will be deleted if customers do not purchase it during this period. If you want your product(s) to remain on yctmarket for more than a month, then use the contact button on the navigation bar to contact yctmarket's admin.

* You can post as many products as you wish. Maximum duration of each product being displayed on yctmarket is a month.

* Payments will be recieved on delivery.



Customer

* You will be contacted as soon as you show interest on a product by entering your contact details in the form provided.

* You will be notified about the product if its still available or not. In a situation when the product is unavailable you can still check on yctmarket for similar products.

* Note that delivery payment is not included in the product amount as this will be discussed with the seller based on your location.

* Payments should be made on delivery.



Adverts

* For advert placements, use the contact button on the navigation bar to contact yctmarket's admin for the procedures of placing advert on yctmaret.
